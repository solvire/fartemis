- **Headquarters**: 620 Crossroads Blvd, Cary, NC 27518
- **Employees**: Over 1,000 in Cary, with more than 40 offices worldwide

- **Founded**: 1991
- **Industry**: Video game and software development

## Core Business

Epic Games operates across several key business areas:

1. **Game Development**: Creator of popular game franchises like Fortnite (their most successful title), Gears of War (now owned by Microsoft), and Unreal Tournament.

2. **Unreal Engine**: Developer of one of the world's most successful game engines, which is used not only for video games but has expanded to film, automotive, and other industries.

3. **Epic Games Store**: A digital distribution platform that competes with Steam and other game marketplaces.

4. **Epic Online Services**: Provides developers with tools to build, distribute, and operate games and other digital content.
